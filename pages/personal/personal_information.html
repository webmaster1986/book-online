Booking Policies

Confirmation and Payment: Provisional reservations will be kept for a maximum of 14 days (subject to room demand). * Written confirmation is needed within 14 days of reservation, failing which Ellerman House reserves the right to cancel the reservation. * 20% non-refundable deposit of accommodation is required on receipt of written confirmation.* The full balance of accommodation is payable six weeks prior to the date of arrival.

Cancellations: All bookings confirmed in writing will be liable for 20% of full accommodation if cancelled earlier than 31 days of arrival. *50% of full accommodation is payable if cancellations are made within 30 days of arrival. * The full accommodation account is payable if cancellations are made within seven days of date of arrival. * In the event of premature departure the full duration of the stay, as originally booked and confirmed, will apply. *Refunds of deposits/prepayments for bookings are at the discretion of management and are dependent on the ability to re-let the room/s.
